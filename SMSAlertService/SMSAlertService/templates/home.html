What is the SMS Alert Service?

We constantly scan online marketplaces and alert our users (via SMS) when items on their wishlist become available. See the "Supported Markets" tab on this page to check which ones we cover.


How do I use it?

Once you've signed up, just add keywords to your profile and buy some alerts. That's it! When someone makes a new post in a Supported Market that contains one or more of those keywords, we'll text you a link to the post, so you don't miss out.

Tips

Computers take everything you say very literally, so be sure to structure your keywords in a way that's not too general, but not too narrow either. Here's some tips and tricks to get the most out of the service.

  • There is no limit on how many keywords you can use.
  • Using broad terms like "magazine" in your keywords might waste alerts on things you don't need or want.
  • However, if you use "Glock magazine" as your only keyword, you won't get notified if the seller's post says something like "Glock mag" or even "Glock magazines."
  • The best strategy is to add multiple keywords for the same item. For example: "Glock mag", "Glock mags", "Glock magazine", "Glock magazines", "G19 mag", "G19 mags", "G19 magazine", and so on.
  • You'll receive a maximum of one sms alert per post, regardless of how many of your keywords are present in that post.
