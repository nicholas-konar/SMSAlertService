Welcome to the SMS Alert Service

What is the SMS Alert Service?

The SMS Alert Service scans online marketplaces, and alerts you (via SMS) the instant an item you want is posted for sale.

Check the Supported Markets tab to the right to see which ones we cover.

How do I use it?

Once you've signed up and purchased some alerts, all you need to do is add keywords on your profile page and wait by the phone.

Remember, we alert you to every post that contains any of your keywords, so you don't have to jump on the first reasonably priced item you see. With the SMS Alert Service, you have the luxury of holding out for the best deals and saving money.

Tips

Computers only do exactly what you tell them. Structure your keywords so that each entry isn't too broad, and use several keywords to describe the same thing. You're essentially guessing which words or phrases a seller is going to use in their post.

Here's some tips and tricks to get the most out of the service.

  • There is no limit on how many keywords you can use.
  • Using broad terms like "magazine" in your keywords might waste alerts on things you don't want.
  • However, if you used "Glock magazine" as your only keyword, you wouldn't get notified if the seller's post says "Glock mag" or even "Glock magazines."
  • The best strategy is to add multiple keywords for the same item. For example: "Glock mag", "Glock mags", "Glock magazine", "Glock magazines", "G19 mag", "G19 mags", "G19 magazine", and so on.
  • You'll receive a maximum of one SMS Alert per post, regardless of how many of your keywords are present in that post - so use as many as you need to!
