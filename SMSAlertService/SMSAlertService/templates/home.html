Welcome to the SMS Alert Service

What is the SMS Alert Service?

The SMS Alert Service scans online marketplaces, and alerts you (via SMS) the instant an item you want is posted for sale.

Check the Supported Markets tab to the right to see which ones we cover.

How do I use it?

New accounts are given 5 alerts to try the service for free. Once you've signed up, simply add keywords on your profile page to begin receiving alerts.

If you love the service, you can purchase more alerts on your profile page, or shop with our Sponsors to get Promo Codes!

If you're hunting a specific item, the SMS Alert Service is the weapon of choice.

Tips

Computers can only do exactly what you tell them to do, and do not account for human logic on their own. You are guessing which words or phrases a seller might use in their post.

Here's some tips and tricks to get the most out of the service.

  • Keywords are not case-sensitive.
  • There is no limit on how many keywords you can use.
  • The service automatically accounts for the plural form of your keywords.
  • You'll receive a maximum of one SMS Alert per post, regardless of how many of your keywords we found in that post - so use as many as you need to!
  • Avoid very broad terms like "magazine" so you don't waste alerts on things you don't want.
  • If you used "Glock magazine" as your only keyword, you wouldn't get notified if the seller's post says "Glock mag"
  • The best strategy is to add a series of keywords for the same item. For example: "Glock mag", "Glock magazine", "G19 mag", "G19 magazine", and so on. (Press Enter after each phrase in the quotations)
