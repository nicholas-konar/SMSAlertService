Welcome to the SMS Alert Service

What is the SMS Alert Service?

The SMS Alert Service watches r/GunAccessoriesForSale and alerts you (via SMS) the instant an item you want is posted for sale.

You tell the service which items you want by adding keywords in your profile page.

If you're hunting a specific item, the SMS Alert Service is the weapon of choice.

How do I use it?

Once you've signed up, simply add keywords on your profile page to begin receiving alerts. The next time a new post is made that contains one of your keywords, we immediately send you an SMS Alert!

New accounts begin with 5 free alerts. If you love the service, you can purchase more alerts from your profile page.

Tips

Here's some tips and tricks to get the most out of the service.

  • Ask yourself, "What words or phrases would a seller use in their post?"
  • The best strategy is to add a series of keywords for each item you want. For example, if you need some Glock mags, use keywords: "Glock mag", "Glock magazine", "G19 mag", "G19 magazine", and so on. (Press Enter after each phrase in quotations)
  • Keywords are not case-sensitive.
  • There is no limit on how many keywords you can use.
  • The service automatically accounts for the plural form of your keywords.
  • You'll receive a max of one SMS Alert per post, regardless of how many of your keywords we found in that post - so use as many as you need to!
