What is the SMS Alert Service?

The SMS Alert Service continuously scans r/GunAccessoriesForSale and alerts you (via SMS) the instant an item you want is posted for sale.

You tell the service which items you want by adding keywords in your profile page.

If you're hunting a specific item, the SMS Alert Service is the weapon of choice.

Why SMS Alert Service?

The SMS Alert Service is purpose-built just for the GAFS community. We know there are free Reddit extensions on the market. However, we found that they were all too slow, too unreliable, and not ideal for a place like GAFS. This service is tailored to the specific needs of GAFS members, and is designed to save you money.

SMS Alert Service is built to be fast, reliable, and easy to use. You'll get notified within ~5 seconds of the post hitting Reddit.
