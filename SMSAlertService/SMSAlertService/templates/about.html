What is the SMS Alert Service?


We're the "in stock notification" provider for the following buy/sell/trade subreddits:

r/GunAccessoriesForSale

This service is for users who may be too busy to constantly check Reddit, are after a rare or uncommon item, or just need an item fast! To use the service, simply:

  • Create an Account
  • Buy an SMS Package
  • Enter your Keywords

The next time a post contains one of your keywords, we'll send you a text to let you know it's available!

Anyone caught using our service for scalping, flipping, hoarding, etc. will have their account terminated and number blacklisted.
