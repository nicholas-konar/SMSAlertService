Privacy Policy

We take privacy seriously.

Choosing between convenient services and your privacy? Not a chance. The SMS Alert Service made all design choices with respect to your operational security.

  • We do not collect device fingerprints, IP addresses, names, addresses, or emails.

  • We do not sell or share our user's information for any reason.
  • We do not cooperate with law enforcement without a warrant.
